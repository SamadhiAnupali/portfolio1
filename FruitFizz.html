SMOOTHIES Website

Project Description - Smoothies Web Application with 3D Carousel Using Smart Animate

Role: UI/UX Design Team Member

As a UI/UX Designer, I spearheaded the creation of the captivating home screen design aimed at providing an engaging and user-friendly entry point for visitors to the website. The home screen served as a pivotal interface, presenting a seamless fusion of aesthetics and functionality to deliver an immersive user experience.

Key Responsibilities and Achievements

  • Conceptualized and crafted an intuitive home screen layout, focusing on user-centric design principles to enhance usability.
  • Implemented modern design trends and innovative features, ensuring the home screen reflected the brand's identity and message effectively.

Impact:

The designed home screen served as the digital gateway, enticing visitors with its aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly interface. Its intuitive layout and engaging design elements not only welcomed users but also established a strong first impression, fostering positive user engagement and interaction.
